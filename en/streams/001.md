## Key Takeaways

- Launching an online community for Ukrainian IT professionals worldwide
- Idea grew from Zengineers.Community — weekly Thursday meetups in Zaporizhzhia (2017–2021)
- After 2022 the community scattered globally, old connections broke — this project fills that void
- Format: every Friday, 20:00 London / 22:00 Kyiv, ~40 min
- IT is not only code: career, relocation, burnout — equally important topics
- Community name vote — **winner: ІТ П'ятниця** (>50% of votes). Candidates: Friday DevPub · Свої в ІТ · DevPub · May the Friday be with you · Placeholder
