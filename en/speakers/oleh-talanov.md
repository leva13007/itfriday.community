CTO and co-founder of **WebCap Digitals** — helping Seed/Series A startups accelerate development velocity on the TALL stack (Tailwind, Alpine.js, Livewire, Laravel). Also co-founder of death-metal band **Hell:ON** (Zaporizhzhia, 2005–present, 20+ years, 7+ albums, toured with Kreator, Napalm Death, Annihilator). Non-linear career path: electrical engineering → civil service → design → frontend → CTO. Three-time co-founder: the band, a digital agency (Impression Bureau, closed 2021), and a tech company (WebCap, launched in the first week of lockdown, March 2020). Lecturer at IT Academy (IMT), Zaporizhzhia.
