# Become a Speaker

Want to speak at IT Friday? Welcome — that's how most topics happen.

## Who this is for

You don't need to be a "recognized expert". A concrete case, a bug you spent three days chasing, or a tool that surprised you is already enough.

Formats range from technical deep-dives and live coding to career stories or interviews. Full list — in [Wiki → Stream Formats](/en/wiki/formats).

## How to apply

1. Message us on [Telegram](https://t.me/+QRJNFfHaLxMwYzcy) or in Discord, `#ideas` channel
2. Briefly describe the topic and format — 2-3 sentences is enough, no slides needed upfront
3. The host replies personally and aligns on details

::: tip Deadline
A request to speak at the upcoming Friday must come in **by Tuesday inclusive**. Anything from Wednesday on automatically goes to the following Friday, not this one. Adjustments are possible by agreement, but this is the baseline rule.
:::

## What happens next

Once the topic and date are confirmed, the host collects the needed info (name, role, LinkedIn, short bio) closer to the stream, and after the broadcast you get a profile in [Speakers](/en/speakers).
