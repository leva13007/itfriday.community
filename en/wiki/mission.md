# Mission

**Online community for Ukrainian IT professionals worldwide — live, technical, and human in equal measure.**

## Origin

In 2017, Zaporizhzhia had a thriving IT meetup — Zengineers.Community.
Weekly Thursday gatherings, two speakers, live conversation.
It opened the door to IT — through networking, talks, and a sense of belonging.

After COVID and the full-scale invasion of 2022, the community ceased to exist.
People scattered across the world. Connections broke.

**This project is the answer to that void.**

## Mission

Bring Ukrainian IT professionals together — regardless of country — around a shared space where:

- You can learn something new about technology (without excessive academia)
- You can talk about real IT life — career, relocation, burnout
- You can find people on the same wavelength
- You can be a speaker or a listener — both roles are equal
- You can share not only successes but also failures and hard times

## What this is NOT

- ❌ Not a course or bootcamp
- ❌ Not a recruitment channel
- ❌ Not a conference with a programme committee
- ❌ Not an attempt to revive Zengineers or compete with other communities
